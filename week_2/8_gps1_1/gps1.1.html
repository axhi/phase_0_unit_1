Peer Pairing Information

What is peer-paring?

Together, discuss and describe why you think pair-programming is important to DBC. Code up what you've discussed according to the wireframe you made. Also discuss what pairing means and define driver and navigator. You will want to compare these on the website.

Why peer pairing

Why peer pairing body copy

  • Better design process through colaboration
  • Reduction of errors
  • Collaboration reduces process time

Steps to take while peer-paring

Steps to take for a successful peer-programming session

  1. Greet your pair and find out how they are doing today
  2. See if there is anything that may affect your pair's ability to program today
  3. Discuss who will begin as the driver and who will take the role of navigator at first (be sure to share these duties!)
